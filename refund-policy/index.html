Refund Policy

Our commitment to your satisfaction

Introduction

At Helipad, we strive to provide exceptional services and ensure our clients are fully satisfied with their experience. However, we understand that situations may arise where a refund is necessary. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be granted and the procedures for requesting a refund.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

a. Service Dissatisfaction

If you are not satisfied with our recruitment services, you may be eligible for a refund within the first 30 days of the service commencement. To qualify, you must demonstrate that the services provided did not meet the agreed-upon standards or expectations outlined in our service agreement.

b. Cancellations

Clients who cancel their service agreement before the recruitment process begins may be eligible for a full refund. If the recruitment process has already commenced, a partial refund may be granted, deducting any costs incurred up to the point of cancellation.

2. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be granted under the following conditions:

  • The client changes their mind after the recruitment process is completed.
  • The client fails to provide necessary information or cooperation required for the recruitment process.
  • The client violates the terms and conditions outlined in the service agreement.

3. Refund Process

To request a refund, follow these steps:

  1. Submit a Refund Request: Contact us via email at hello@tryhelipad.com with the subject line "Refund Request." Include your name, contact information, service details, and a detailed explanation of the reason for the refund request.
  2. Review and Assessment: Upon receiving your refund request, our team will review and assess the situation. We may contact you for additional information or clarification if needed.
  3. Decision Notification: We will notify you of our decision regarding your refund request within 10 business days. If approved, we will process the refund within 14 business days of the notification.

4. Partial Refunds

In cases where a partial refund is granted, the amount refunded will be proportional to the unused portion of the service and any costs already incurred by Helipad during the recruitment process.

5. Payment Method

Refunds will be issued using the same payment method originally used for the purchase. If this is not possible, an alternative method will be arranged.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or need further assistance regarding our Refund Policy, please contact us at:
