Frequently Asked Questions

What is Helipad?

+

Helipad is a virtual staffing agency that helps you hire top global talent for a fraction of the cost.

How do I get started?

+

You can get started by getting an instant quote on our website and paying the $100 initiation fee.

What roles can I hire for?

+

We offer a wide range of roles including developers, designers, virtual assistants, and more.

How much does it cost?

+

Our services start at $4 per hour for remote talent with significant cost savings over local hires.

Is there a guarantee?

+

Yes, we offer a 6-month money-back guarantee if you aren't happy with our service.

How quickly can I hire someone?

+

Our process is very quick. You can have a new team member working on your projects within a week.

How do you ensure the quality of the talent?

+

We rigorously vet our candidates through a comprehensive recruitment process, ensuring they meet our high standards of excellence and have over 2 years of industry experience.

What if the candidate is not a good fit?

+

If the candidate is not a good fit, we will provide additional candidates until we find the perfect match for your needs. Your satisfaction is our priority.

How do I communicate with my virtual staff?

+

You can communicate with your virtual staff through various channels such as email, video calls, and project management tools. We ensure they are equipped with the necessary tools to integrate seamlessly into your workflow.

Can I hire part-time or full-time staff?

+

Yes, you can hire both part-time and full-time staff based on your business needs. We offer flexible staffing solutions to suit your requirements.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards and bank transfers. Our payment process is secure and hassle-free.

Why should I choose Helipad over other staffing agencies?

+

We offer unparalleled cost savings, access to the top 1% of global talent, a rigorous vetting process, and a 6-month money-back guarantee. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction sets us apart.
